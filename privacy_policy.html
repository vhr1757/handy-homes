Privacy Policy

At Handy Homes, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us, such as your name, contact information, and service preferences when you use our platform or contact us.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us improve our services, process transactions, communicate with you, and enhance your overall experience with Handy Homes.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share it with trusted partners who help us operate our website and services, under confidentiality agreements.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance user experience and track website performance. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or misuse.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us via the Contact Us page.

7. Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of our website and services indicates your acceptance of any updates.
